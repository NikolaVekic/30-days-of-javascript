Frequently Asked Questions

What is HTML?

HTML is the backbone of every website. Without it, there’s no content, think of it as the blueprint for building your digital masterpiece.

What's the difference between CSS and JavaScript?

CSS is your website's stylist, making it look sharp and attractive. JavaScript is the life of the party, adding interactivity and pizzazz.

How do I optimize my website for performance?

Stop clogging your site with bulky files and massive images. Compress, cache, and clean up your code—speed is the name of the game.

What is responsive design?

Responsive design ensures your site looks great on any device. It’s like your website wearing a perfectly tailored suit—always in style, everywhere.
